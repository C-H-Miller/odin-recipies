Vegan Crispy Treats

The OG best thing to do with boring ass cereal



Ingredients:

  • One (10 ounce) package of Dandies® Mini Marshmallows
  • 4 T vegan butter of choice
  • 1 T water
  • 4.5 cups Crisp Rice Cereal

Steps:

  1. In a large pot melt vegan butter over medium to low heat.
  2. Once melted add Dandies®, stirring constantly.
  3. After 3 minutes add 1 T water and continue stirring until melted together.
  4. Remove from heat
  5. Add crisp rice cereal to melted marshmallow mixture and mix to fully coat the cereal. Press mixture into a 9x9 pan
  6. Once cooled, cut into squares, and enjoy!
