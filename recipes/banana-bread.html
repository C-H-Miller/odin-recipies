Banana Bread

This is the best Bread that is made with old stanky bananas that your husband couldn't eat before they turned brown!

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe defrosted bananas
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 and 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/3 cup of oil or melted butter
  • Optional: Chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Set oven to preheat at 350 degrees
  2. Mix sugar into bananas in a large bowl
  3. Slowly add other wet ingredients
  4. Stir in dry ingredients, adding them slowly
  5. Grease a pan and add the mixture
  6. Toss that shit in the oven, then poke after 40ish minutes
  7. If there is no wet batter on stick, it's good to go!
  8. Eat it all in one go, feel no shame
